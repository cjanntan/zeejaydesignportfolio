Hi, I'm Anantha !

A Product Designer, tech enthusiast, and visual artist based in Bengaluru. With 4.6 years of professional experience, I specialize in crafting intuitive, user-centered digital experiences that seamlessly bridge design and technology.

Currently Working at Telyport Technologies. I focus on solving complex problems through thoughtful design, creating solutions that are both functional and engaging. My approach is driven by a passion for storytelling, a love for problem solving, and a deep curiosity about how things work.

At the heart of my work is a desire to create emotional experiences at the intersection of art, design, and AI bringing human depth to digital interactions.

Whether it's refining the details of a user interface or conceptualizing a bold new feature, I aim to bring clarity, creativity, and empathy to every project I take on.
